# CHAPTER 10: TOUCH SENSOR

- ESP32 offers up to 10 capacitive touch GPIO.
- Mechanical switches are prone to jitter that must be eliminated when used, BUT this is not the case with ESP32's built-in touch sensor.


# Project 10.1: Read the Touch Sensor

The ESP32's touch sensor:

    - Supports up to 10 GPIO channels as capacitive touch pins:
    - Each pin can be used separately as an independent touch switch, or be combined to produce multiple touch points.
    - The electrical signals generated by touch are analog data, and get converted by an internal ADC converter.
    - All touch pins have ADC functionality.
